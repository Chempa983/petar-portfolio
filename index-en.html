Projects / Case studies

Case: Improved communication between 4 departments

Background: Communication and information flow were weak between multiple departments, causing delays and unnecessary stop-time in daily operations.

  • Action: Established clearer contact routes and direct numbers per department, plus a simpler way of sharing operational information.
  • Impact: Faster responses and less time wasted “finding the right person”, resulting in smoother flow and better collaboration.
  • Lesson learned: Small, clear communication standards can significantly improve operations and work environment.

Note: I did not have a formal leadership title – I drove the improvement operationally together with colleagues.

Case: Focused forklift resources to the right area

Background: Forklift drivers were spread across too many tasks, which reduced accuracy and meant critical work was not completed on time.

  • Action: Clarified priorities and proposed a more dedicated allocation of forklift resources, with clearer responsibility per area.
  • Impact: Better focus, less fragmentation and a more predictable internal flow/delivery.
  • Lesson learned: Clear ownership + priorities beats “everyone helps with everything”.

I can explain the setup further in an interview (what changed, how it was communicated, and how we followed up).
